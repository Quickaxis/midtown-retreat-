# Walkthrough - Midtown Retreat Homestay (Apple-Inspired Redesign)

A premium, high-end visual overhaul of the **Midtown Retreat Homestay** website, designed with an Apple-inspired aesthetic and bold editorial typography.

## ✨ Refined Design Highlights
- **Playfair Display Typography**: 
    - **Hero**: Massive 100px Playfair Display (weight 800) in italics for a high-fashion, editorial look.
    - **Global Headings**: Switched to Playfair Display (weight 700) for a bolder, more luxurious feel across all sections.
- **High-Transparency Glass**:
    - **Ultra-Clear Navbar**: Reduced opacity (`rgba(255,255,255,0.08)`) with heavy 20px blur and sharp white text for a sophisticated floating effect.
    - **Refined Hero Card**: Transparent glass (`rgba(255,255,255,0.12)`) with white text, harmonizing with the main navbar.
- **Hero Polish**: Bold subtext (DM Sans 700) at 18px for better clarity and impact.
- **Apple Aesthetic**: Warm off-white background (`#f8f6f2`) with a heavy emphasis on glassmorphism and soft shadows.
